Why We Built This

"I bought SOL at $200. I told myself I'd hold for five years. Then the crash hit $15, and I panic-sold everything. Six months later, SOL was back at $100. The problem was never the price — it was me. I couldn't stop myself from making the worst possible decision at the worst possible moment. So I built the thing I wish had existed."

Time Lock SOL isn't about trust in us. It's about removing yourself from the equation. The smart contract holds your SOL in a program-derived address. No admin key can override it. No emergency button exists. The blockchain is the only judge — and it doesn't feel fear.

This is for everyone who ever sold too early. For the next generation who won't have to. Lock it. Forget it. Be rewarded by time.
